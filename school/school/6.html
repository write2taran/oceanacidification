Short summary of Ocean Acidification

"Destroy the Ocean and you will destroy 70% of earth"- Anonymous

It's well said that if you change one thing in an environment, there can be many repercussions throughout the whole ecosystem. Ocean Acidifiaction is an important driver of change in biological and ecological systems. It refers to the modication of water's chemistry, which has devastating effect on all organisms living in marine environment and ultimately on those too which depend on it. Some of the ill effects are listed on this portal but the potential effects have been predicted to be even more hazardous.
