How Ocean Acidification Occurs

(http://www.oceanacidification.org.uk)

  1. CO2 released from several sources like Cars and Industries, dissolves in water.


  2. CO2 reacts with water to form carbonic acid.


  3. Carbonic acid dissolves rapidly to form H+ ions (an acid) and bicarbonate, HCO-3(a base).

  4. Seawater is naturally saturated with another base, carbonate ion (CO3-2) that acts like an antacid to neutralize the H+, forming more bicarbonate.

  5. Thus Seawater is actually becoming neutral
